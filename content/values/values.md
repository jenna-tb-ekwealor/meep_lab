##  Our Core Values: Community, Collaboration, and Curiosity
<br>
*Community*
The MEEP Lab places a high value on community, where diverse voices are
supported, heard, and valued. We aim to foster an environment where everyone feels a sense
of belonging and support, and we are committed to creating a safe space where all lab
members can freely express their identities, values, and cultures. By nurturing a welcoming and
inclusive atmosphere, we not only strengthen our scientific endeavors but also build a
supportive network that extends beyond your time in this lab.

*Collaboration*
Collaboration is at the heart of our scientific efforts. We believe that working
together, sharing diverse perspectives, and pooling our expertise leads to greater innovation
and discovery. Our lab culture believes in teamwork, open communication, and mutual respect,
and we strive to ensure that all contributions are valued and recognized. Through collaborative
efforts, we aim to tackle challenges and achieve our research goals more effectively.

*Curiosity*
Wisdom begins with wonder.” Curiosity drives our passion for science and fuels our
intellectual engagement. We embrace a growth mindset, encouraging continuous learning, self-
reflection, and the pursuit of excellence. In our lab, curiosity is not just about seeking answers
but also about fostering an environment where questioning, exploring new ideas, and
embracing challenges are integral to our work. We support each other's aspirations and strive to
create opportunities for personal and professional growth, believing that a curious mind is key to
advancing knowledge and achieving success. We encourage self-reflection and the pursuit of
excellence in personal goals, viewing our role as supportive rather than directive.

*These values are aspirational and require ongoing effort and communication to fully embody.
We advocate for humility, honesty, and compassion as we work towards these ideals together.*
